# Executive Brief: Hektor Vector Database

## One-Page Summary

**What:** High-performance open-source vector database for AI/ML applications  
**Why:** $2.5B market growing at 45% CAGR, fragmented competitive landscape  
**How:** Superior performance (50-70% faster) + lower costs (60-80% TCO savings)  
**Investment:** $15M Series A for go-to-market scale  
**Return:** $30M ARR by 2027, $155M by 2029 (base case)

## Market Opportunity

- **Market Size:** $2.5B in 2026, $8B+ by 2029
- **Growth Rate:** 45-50% CAGR
- **Drivers:** AI/ML adoption, RAG architecture, semantic search
- **Our Focus:** Self-hosted and enterprise deployments (35% of market)

## Competitive Advantage

**Performance:**
- 2.9ms p99 latency (50-70% faster than competitors)
- Billion-scale proven (96.8% recall at 1B vectors)
- SIMD optimization (AVX-512 support)

**Innovation:**
- Industry-first perceptual quantization
- 40-60% memory reduction
- Local ONNX embeddings

**Economics:**
- 60-80% lower TCO vs. managed services
- Open source (no vendor lock-in)
- Flexible deployment (cloud, on-prem, edge)
