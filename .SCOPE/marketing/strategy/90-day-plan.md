## Risk Mitigation

**Risk**: Content saturation, audience fatigue  
**Mitigation**: Vary content types, focus on value over volume

**Risk**: Technical claims challenged  
**Mitigation**: Over-document, benchmark transparency, reproducible results

**Risk**: Competitor response (feature parity)  
**Mitigation**: Focus on spectral differentiation, move faster, deeper expertise

**Risk**: Resource constraints  
**Mitigation**: Templates ready, automation where possible, prioritize high-impact
