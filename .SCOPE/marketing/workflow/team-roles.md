## Collaboration Workflows

### Content Approval Process
1. **Draft**: Creator writes, saves to shared drive
2. **Review**: Manager reviews (24hr turnaround)
3. **Design**: Designer adds visuals (12hr turnaround)
4. **Technical Review**: Dev Relations checks accuracy (if technical)
5. **Publish**: Creator publishes, logs in calendar

**Expedited**: Skip review for pre-approved templates (social posts from library)

### Weekly Meeting Agenda
**When**: Monday 10am, 30 minutes

**Agenda**:
1. Last week review (10 min)
   - What worked / what didn't
   - Top performers / learnings
2. This week plan (10 min)
   - Content calendar confirmation
   - Special campaigns/announcements
3. Blockers & asks (5 min)
   - What do you need from team?
4. Quick wins / ideas (5 min)
   - Opportunities spotted

**Action Items**: Documented in shared doc, owner assigned

### Emergency Protocol
**For**: Breaking news, urgent issues, major opportunities

**Process**:
1. Alert team in Slack #marketing-urgent
2. Manager decides: act now or schedule
3. If act now: expedited approval (30 min)
4. Post-mortem after (what went well/improve)

## Communication Norms

### Response Times
- Slack: 2 hours during work hours
- Email: 24 hours
- External (Twitter/LinkedIn): 4 hours for @mentions

### Escalation
- **Level 1**: Creator handles (standard comments, questions)
- **Level 2**: Manager handles (complaints, partnership inquiries)
- **Level 3**: Leadership handles (major issues, press)

### Documentation
- All campaigns documented in shared folder
- Learnings captured in weekly reports
- Assets organized by campaign/date
- Analytics tracked in dashboard
