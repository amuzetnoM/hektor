# 90-Day Content Calendar
> **Daily posting schedule with all content mapped**

## How to Use This Calendar

1. Check today's date
2. Find the scheduled content
3. Use pre-written copy from respective files
4. Post at optimal times (see guidelines below)
5. Track engagement metrics

---

## Optimal Posting Times

**LinkedIn**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 5-6pm local time  
**Twitter**: Monday-Friday, 9am, 12pm, or 5pm local time  
**GitHub**: Anytime (async community)  
**Reddit/HN**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-12pm PT

## Daily Workflow

### Morning (9am)
1. Check yesterday's engagement
2. Respond to all comments
3. Prepare today's content
4. Schedule posts (or post manually)

### Midday (12pm)
1. Monitor post performance
2. Engage with replies
3. Share to relevant groups/forums

### Evening (5-6pm)
1. Final engagement check
2. Document learnings
3. Adjust next day if needed

**Week 1-2**: Establish presence (impressions, reach)  
**Week 3-4**: Build engagement (comments, shares)  
**Week 5-8**: Drive conversions (clicks, signups)
